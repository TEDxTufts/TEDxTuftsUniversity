TEDxTuftsUniversity Speakers

Apply To Speak.

Have an idea you want to share with the Tufts community? Are you a writer, performer, or artist who would like to tell your story to Tufts students, faculty, staff, and alumni? We want you to apply to speak at TEDxTuftsUniversity 2015. The application goes live Wednesday, January 22, 2015.

Nominate Someone.

We need your help to find bright and inspiring minds across campus and beyond. TEDxTuftsUniversity, more than anything, is about Tufts: we want to showcase innovative and inspiring ideas that come from our university. If you know a student, teacher, or alumni with an amazing idea, let us know!
